Hi, I'm Rachel!
I'm a user experience and user interface designer who loves to create intuitive designs and fulfilling user experiences.
For me, design is a culmination of my various interests in art, human psychology, and front-end development. Creating pretty vector illustrations is fun, but sometimes I also like turning those images into real websites I can play around with myself. What's the *most* fun for me though is watching other people have fun with my creations and hearing about what they think!
Outside of design things, I also like to dance (mostly to hip-hop and kpop), watch cooking videos, occasionally take pictures, and discover/jam to music. Recently I've been particularly interested in things that connect music and art - like music videos and album covers!
